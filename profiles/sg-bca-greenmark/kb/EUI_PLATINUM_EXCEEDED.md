BCA Green Mark 2021, Section 4.1 — Energy Use Intensity (EUI)

EUI measures the total energy consumed by a building per unit of gross floor area per year (kWh/m²/year). For BCA Green Mark Platinum certification, the EUI must not exceed 115 kWh/m²/year.

EUI is calculated from 12 months of metered electricity and gas consumption data. The clarus edge daemon collects hourly energy readings, aggregates them over the audit period, and divides by the building's certified gross floor area.

A breach of this threshold does not automatically fail certification — the BCA assessor may accept documented mitigating factors — but it must be disclosed in the Section 4 submission and is flagged as HIGH severity by the clarus rule engine.
