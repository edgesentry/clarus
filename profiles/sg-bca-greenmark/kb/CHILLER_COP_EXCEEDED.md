BCA Green Mark 2021, Section 4.2 — Chiller Plant Efficiency

Chiller plant efficiency is measured in kW/RT (kilowatts of input power per refrigeration ton of cooling output). Lower values indicate more efficient plant. For Platinum certification, the overall chiller plant efficiency must not exceed 0.65 kW/RT.

The clarus edge daemon reads chiller input power (kW) and cooling output (RT) from the BMS integration point at 15-minute intervals. The efficiency value reported is the 12-month average weighted by cooling load.
