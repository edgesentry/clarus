BCA Green Mark 2021, Section 4.3 — Lighting Power Density (LPD)

LPD measures the installed lighting power per unit of gross floor area (W/m²). For Platinum certification in retail and commercial spaces, LPD must not exceed 15 W/m².

The clarus edge daemon reads installed lighting power from the electrical sub-metering system. LPD is calculated as the total connected lighting load divided by the certified gross floor area. Emergency and display lighting are excluded per BCA measurement guidelines.
